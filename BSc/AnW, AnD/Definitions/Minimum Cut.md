## Symbol

C ^symbol
## Definition

For [[Weighted Graph]] $G=(V,E),\ w : E \to \mathbb{R}$ or $w(e) = 1$, the minimum cut $C \subseteq E$ is the cut with the least $\Sigma_{e \in C}\ w(e)$ ^definition
