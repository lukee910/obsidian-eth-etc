## 2.2 Quasilinear equations

General form of a first order quasilinear PDE in two variables:

$$
	a(x, y, u)u_x + b(x, y, u) u_y = c(x, y, u)
$$
The input of $u$ to $a, b, c$ indicates the quasilinearity. $a, b, c$ cannot derive $u$ further.
