## Symbol

Cauchy Problem ^symbol
## Definition

A PDE plus an initial value ^definition
Usually has the following form:
$$
\left\{
	\begin{array}{lr}
		u(x, t) = 0 & \text{The PDE} \\
		u(x, 0) = g(x) & \text{(A number of) initial values}
	\end{array}
\right.
$$
