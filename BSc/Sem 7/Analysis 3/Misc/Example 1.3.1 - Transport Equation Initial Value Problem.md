Consider [[Transport Equation]] $u_t + c u_x = 0$. Here, $u$ can represent the concentration of a pollutant in a river at time $t$ and position $x$. $c$ represents the velocity of the river.

[[Ana3 - Cauchy Problem]]:

$$
\left\{
	\begin{array}{lr}
		u_t + cu_x = 0 & (x, t) \in \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R}^+ \\
		u(x, 0) = g(x) & x \in \mathbb{R}
	\end{array}
\right.
$$
