## Thm 2.3.1
For each matrix $A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times m}$, there exists orthogonal matrices $U \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}$ and $V \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times m}$ such that $A$ can be expressed as

$$
\begin{align}
	A = U \Sigma V^T,\ \Sigma = diag(\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_{\min(n,m)}),\ \sigma_i \geq \sigma_{i+1}
\end{align}
$$
