## Thm 1.4.7

Any symmetric and positive semidefinite matrix $\Sigma$ can be non-negatively diagonalized with an orthogonal matrix

$$
\begin{align*}
	\Sigma = Q \Lambda Q^T,\ \Lambda = diag(\lambda_1,\ldots,\lambda_n),\\ \lambda_1 \geq \ldots \geq \lambda_n \geq 0,\ Q \text{ orthogonal}.
\end{align*}
$$
