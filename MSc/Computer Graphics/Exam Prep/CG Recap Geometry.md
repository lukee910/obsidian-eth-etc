## Trigonometric Functions
### Unit Circle
$$
\begin{array}{c|cccccc}
deg & 0° & 30°                  & 45°                  & 60°                  & 90°             & 180°  \\
\hline
rad & 0  & \frac{\pi}{6}      & \frac{\pi}{4}      & \frac{\pi}{3}      & \frac{\pi}{2} & \pi \\
cos & 1  & \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} & \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} & \frac{1}{2}        & 0               & -1    \\
sin & 0  & \frac{1}{2}        & \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} & \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} & 1               & 0     \\
tan & 0  & \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} & 1                    & \sqrt{3}           & +\infty       & 0     \\
\end{array}
$$
$\sin$ order:
$$
\sin(): 0,\frac{\pi}{6},\frac{\pi}{4},\frac{\pi}{2},\pi = \frac{\sqrt{0}}{2}, \frac{\sqrt{1}}{2},\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2},\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \frac{\sqrt{4}}{2}
$$

## Cross Product
$$
\begin{align}
\vec{a} \times \vec{b} &= \begin{pmatrix}
  a_{1} \\ a_{2} \\ a_{3}
\end{pmatrix}
\times
\begin{pmatrix}
  b_{1} \\ b_{2} \\ b_{3}
\end{pmatrix} \\
&= \begin{pmatrix}
a_{2}b_{3} - a_{3}b_{2} \\ a_{3}b_{1} - a_{1}b_{3} \\ a_{1}b_{2} - a_{2}b_{1}
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
$$

## Shape Formulas
### Circle
- $A = r^{2} \pi$
- Circumference: $u = 2 r \pi$
### Sphere
- Surface: $A = 4 r^{2} \pi$
- Volume: $V = \frac{4}{3} r^{3} \pi$
