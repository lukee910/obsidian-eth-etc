$$
L_{\lambda}(T) = \frac{2hc^{2}}{\lambda^{5}\left( e^{\frac{hc}{\lambda k_{B} \cdot T}} - 1\right)}
$$
where:
$$
\begin{align}
c &\approx 2.998 \times 10^{9} &\text{m} \cdot \text{s}^{-1} &\quad \text{(speed of light in vacuum)} \\ 
h &\approx 6.626 \times 10^{-34} &\text{J} \cdot \text{s} &\quad \text{(Planck's constant)} \\
k_{B} &\approx 1.381 \times 10^{-23} &\text{J} \cdot \text{K}^{-1} &\quad \text{(Boltzmann constant)}
\end{align}
$$
