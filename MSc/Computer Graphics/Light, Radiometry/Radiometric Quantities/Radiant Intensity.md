$$
I(\overrightarrow{\omega}) = \frac{d\Phi}{d\overrightarrow{\omega}} \quad \left[ \frac{W}{sr} \right]
$$
Directional density of [[Flux]]. [[Flux]] per [[Solid Angle]].
## Relation to Flux
Relation to [[Flux]], especially for light sources.
$$
\begin{align}
\Phi &= \int_{S^{2}} I(\omega)\ d\omega & \\
\Phi &= 4\pi I & \text{(Isotropic point source)}
\end{align}
$$
