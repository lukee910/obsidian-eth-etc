## Idea
Approximation of Maxwell equations for tiny scatters, typically smaller than 1/10 of the wavelength of visible light.
### Uses
Atmospheric scattering, gasses, transparent solids.
## Scattering Coefficient
$$
\sigma_{s\text{ Rayleigh}} (\lambda, d, \eta, \rho) = \rho \frac{2\pi^{5}d^{6}}{3 \lambda^{4}} \left( \frac{\eta^{2} - 1}{\eta^{2} + 2} \right)^{2}
$$
- $\lambda$: Wavelength
- $d$: Diameter of scatterers
- $\eta$: Index of refraction
- $\rho$: Density of scatterers
