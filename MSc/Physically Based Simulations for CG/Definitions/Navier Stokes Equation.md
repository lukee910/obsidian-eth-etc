## Incompressible
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} + u \cdot \nabla u + \frac{1}{\rho}\nabla p &= g + \frac{\mu}{\rho}\nabla \cdot \nabla u &(1) \\
\nabla \cdot u &= 0  &(2)
\end{align}
$$
* $u = (u,v,w)$ Velocity
* $\rho$ Density
* $\mu$ Viscosity coefficient
* $g$ Gravitational acceleration
* $p$ Pressure (vector field)
### Two Parts
#### Momentum Equation
Part $(1)$ of the above equation.
#### Incompressibility Condition
Part $(2)$ of the above equation.
