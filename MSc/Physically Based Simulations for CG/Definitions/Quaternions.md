## Definition
A generalization of complex numbers to higher dimensions.
$$
q = w + xi + yj + zk
$$
where $i^{2}= j^{2} = k^{2} = ijk = -1$
Note the $ijk = -1$.

Noted as $q = [w,v]$, where $v$ vector:
$$
v = \begin{bmatrix}
x \\ y \\ z
\end{bmatrix}
$$
